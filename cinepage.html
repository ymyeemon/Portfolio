UI/UX, Mobile App

CinePage

Overview

CinePage is a sleek and user-friendly mobile application designed to enhance your entertainment and reading experiences. It provide features to effortlessly log movies you've watched and books you've read, organize personalized watchlists, and share your thoughts through reviews and ratings.

Product Goal

The goal is to build and provide a seamless mobile experience that allows movies lovers, readers, movie and book enthusiasts to log, review, discuss and meet people who shares the same interest and taste in both categories within the one platform.

Low Fidelity Wireframe

This is the early concept of the app design, highlighting the basic structure, layout patterns and navigation to visualize the user experience in a simple way.

Main Pages

The app's key features are organized across four main tabs such as the Homepage, Profile, Search, and Activity tabs. The Homepage offers a quick access to users' latest entries and recommendations. The Profile tab allows users to manage their personal information, track progress, and view past activity while the Search tab help users explore and discover new movies and books.And, the Activity tab displays logged entries, reviews, and ratings from friends.

Feature Highlights

CinePage provides dedicated pages for Trending Books and Trending Movies, offering users a curated view of what's popular. When users click on either section, they’re presented with visually engaging pages showcasing top-rated movies and books.

Feature Highlights

Each book or movie has a dedicated page that provides comprehensive details, including summaries, ratings, and user reviews. These pages are designed to give users a clear overview of movies and books while offering features to easily add the item to their reading or watchlist.
