UI/UX, Mobile App Design

Taste Trail

Overview

Taste Trail is the ultimate restaurants navigation mobile app that helps you discover the best dining spots, read authentic reviews and navigate effortlessly. With features like showing different modes of transportation to get to the various dining sports, bookmarking favorite restaurants and personalized recommendations.

Moodboard

This moodboard reflects my vision & ideation for the project's look and feel, layouts, colors, functionalities, and design inspirations that align with the atmosphere I want to create for users.

Product Goal

The product goal is to create a comprehensive mobile experience to food enthusiasts, social media users and travelers to navigate seamlessly to various restaurants and dining spots while enabling them to attain detailed information, reviews, personalized routes, recommendations. Hence, streamlining the process of finding and visiting recommended places within one platform.
